Statement Making 2019
—
 UNC Charlotte

Statement Making 2019 was UNCC's third annual digital fabrication fashion show, co-hosted by the CCI and CoAA colleges, featuring student's interpretations of the intersections between fashion, fabrication, and sustainability. UNCC's Makerspaces hosted a variety of workshops in fabrication leading up to the event. The event's program was an origami design inspired by the flyers of UNCC's art department.
