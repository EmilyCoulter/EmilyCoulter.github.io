VaCELLO
—
 personal project

The VaCello is a sleek electric cello design for 3D printing. I was inspired by the Modular Fiddle, which I had printed out before tackling the design of the VaCello. I wanted to make a beautiful, functional, and affordable instrument for people who are excited by what 3D printing can accomplish. It is intended for use by adults who want to try their hand at learning a new instrument, or want to have a cheap, easily replacable, and quiet cello while traveling. A carbon fiber rod down the neck helps the instrument keep its shape, and a small body means the VaCello is much earier to transport than an acoustic cello.
