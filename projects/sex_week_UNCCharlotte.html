SEX WEEK 2020
—
 Sex Week UNC Charlotte

Sex Week UNC Charlotte is a student organization seeking to address the gaps in North Carolina's sex education. Sex Week hosts more than 20 events every Spring, from bystander intervention training and STD testing, to religious panels and queer media shows. I provided a number of graphics for Sex Week UNC Charlotte's 2020 season. I designed a new logo, cultivating a cohesive brand identity ; created 11 event posters; and freshened up the website, earning Sex Week UNC Charlotte the Outstanding Student Organization Website award from UNC Charlotte's Student Involvement group.
